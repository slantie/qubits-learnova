B5 — Full-screen Lesson Player

Phase 6 — Polish, Bug Fixes & Demo Prep
H17 → H18 1 hr ▶
⚠️ Do NOT use this hour for new features. Fix critical bugs only and prep your demo path.
🧪
Full demo run-through
Admin: create course → add video → add quiz → set rewards → publish. Learner: browse → enroll → watch video → attempt quiz → earn points → complete course. Verify reporting reflects the completion.
20 min
🌱
Seed data
3 courses (1 published open, 1 published paid, 1 draft), 2–3 enrolled learner accounts with varied progress levels. Makes reporting look real and populated during demo.
15 min
🐛
Critical bug fixes only
Focus: auth redirect, publish toggle, lesson player loading, quiz points calculation. Ignore all cosmetic issues.
25 min
